ABOUT

Lifebots™ (autonomous companions), created by Paradigm Unity Corporation, were built for a dying world to ease the anguish of a slow extinction. They were revealed after the procreation ban as an emotional companion. People grew to love their Lifebots™, often marrying them. Ownership of a Lifebot™ is contingent upon sustained employment as well as payment. Those who can't pay or find work have their bots repossesed.
